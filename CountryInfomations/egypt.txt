[Egypt]

Egypt, located in North Africa and the Middle East with Cairo as its capital, is celebrated for its ancient history, 
iconic landmarks such as the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx, diverse landscapes along the Nile River, cultural richness, the allure of the Red Sea's coral reefs, 
and the dynamic blend of historic and modern elements in Cairo, making it a captivating destination with a profound impact on global history and culture.
