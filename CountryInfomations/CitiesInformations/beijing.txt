[Beijing]

Beijing is the capital city of the People's Republic of China and one of the most populous cities globally. 
Steeped in history, it serves as a cultural, political, and economic center. 
Beijing is home to iconic landmarks such as the Forbidden City, Tiananmen Square, and the Temple of Heaven. 
The Great Wall of China, one of the world's most famous structures, is also easily accessible from Beijing. 
The city seamlessly blends ancient traditions with modern development, featuring skyscrapers alongside historic hutongs (narrow alleys). 
As the host city of the 2008 Summer Olympics, Beijing has continued to evolve as a vibrant and influential global metropolis.
