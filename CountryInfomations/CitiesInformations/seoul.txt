[Seoul]

Seoul is the capital and largest city of South Korea, situated on the Han River. 
As the economic, cultural, and political heart of the country, Seoul seamlessly blends a rich history with modernity. 
The city is known for its ancient palaces, including Gyeongbokgung and Changdeokgung, and the vibrant traditional markets like Namdaemun and Dongdaemun. 
The dynamic district of Gangnam showcases the city's contemporary and trendy side. Seoul is recognized for its technological advancements, 
reflected in its cutting-edge architecture and efficient public transportation system. 

The city is also a global hub for K-pop, fashion, and cuisine, making it a captivating destination for both locals and international visitors.
