[Giza]

Giza is a city in Egypt, situated on the west bank of the Nile River, near Cairo. 
It is most famous for the Giza Plateau, which is home to the iconic Giza Pyramids—Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure—and the Sphinx. 
The Great Pyramid of Khufu is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. 
Giza is a significant archaeological and historical site, drawing millions of visitors each year to marvel at the ancient wonders 
and explore the mysteries of ancient Egyptian civilization.
