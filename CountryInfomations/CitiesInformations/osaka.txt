[Osaka]

Osaka is a major city in Japan and the capital of Osaka Prefecture. 
Known for its vibrant culture, historical landmarks, and culinary scene, Osaka is often referred to as the "Kitchen of Japan." 
The city is recognized for its delicious street food, including iconic dishes like takoyaki and okonomiyaki. 
Osaka Castle, a symbol of the city's history, and Universal Studios Japan, a popular theme park, are among its notable attractions. 
Osaka is a bustling economic center, and its lively districts such as Dotonbori and Namba showcase a mix of traditional and modern elements. 
The city is also renowned for its welcoming and friendly atmosphere.
