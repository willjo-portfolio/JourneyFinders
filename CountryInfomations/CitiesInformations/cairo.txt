[Cairo]

Cairo is the capital and largest city of Egypt, located on the banks of the Nile River. 
As one of the oldest cities in the world, Cairo has a rich history dating back to ancient times. 
The city is known for its historical landmarks, including the Giza Pyramids, the Sphinx, and the Egyptian Museum. 
Cairo is a vibrant metropolis, combining the old and the new, with bustling markets, medieval Islamic architecture, and a lively street culture. 
It serves as a cultural, economic, and political center in the Arab world, contributing significantly to Egypt's cultural heritage and global influence.
