[Vancouver]

Vancouver, situated on Canada's southwest coast, is a diverse and picturesque city framed by the Pacific Ocean and the Coast Mountain Range. 
Boasting a population of around 631,000 people, the city is an economic powerhouse with a focus on technology, film production, and tourism. 
Vancouver's cultural vibrancy is evident in its culinary scene and diverse population. 
The city offers a wealth of outdoor activities, from hiking in Stanley Park to winter sports in the nearby mountains. 
Known as "Hollywood North," Vancouver is a prominent hub for film and television production. With efficient public transportation, iconic landmarks, 
and a temperate coastal climate, Vancouver stands as a dynamic metropolis celebrated for its natural beauty and cultural richness.
