[Washington, D.C.]

Washington, D.C. (District of Columbia), is the capital city of the United States. 
It is not part of any state but serves as the political and administrative center of the country. 
Home to iconic landmarks such as the White House, the U.S. Capitol, and the Washington Monument, D.C. is a symbol of American democracy. 
The city is known for its museums, government institutions, and historic significance, attracting tourists, diplomats, and policymakers from around the world.
