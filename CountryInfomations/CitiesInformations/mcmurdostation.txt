[McMurdo Station]

While McMurdo Station is not located within the continental United States, it is a key outpost for the U.S. in Antarctica. 
McMurdo Station serves as the largest research station on the continent, supporting scientific research and exploration. 
It is situated on Ross Island and is a hub for activities related to the U.S. Antarctic Program. 
Despite its remote location, McMurdo Station plays a crucial role in advancing scientific understanding of Antarctica and its unique ecosystems.
