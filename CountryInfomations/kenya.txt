[Kenya]

Kenya, situated in East Africa, is characterized by its diverse landscapes, renowned wildlife, and cultural richness. 
Nairobi, the capital city, serves as the economic, political, and cultural hub of the country. 
From the famous Maasai Mara with its extraordinary wildlife and the Great Rift Valley to the coastal regions along the Indian Ocean, 
Kenya offers a variety of stunning natural features. Diverse ethnic groups, including the distinctive Maasai community, contribute to Kenya's cultural heritage. 

The tourism industry thrives on activities like safaris, hiking, and bird watching. Kenya's economy, with a focus on agriculture, tourism, 
and services, positions it as one of the more economically developed countries in East Africa.
